# BwMe Instruction Manual

After installing the APK file, follow the steps below to start using **BwMe**.

---

## 1. Sign Up

Create your account to get started.

* Enter your **name, email, and password**
* Complete the registration form

---

## 2. Login

Access your account.

* Enter your login credentials
* Set your **display name**
* Choose a **profile picture**

---

## 3. Dashboard

View your financial overview.

* Check your **account summary**
* See your **total revenue**
* View **recent expenses**

---

## 4. Manage Profile

Customize and review your profile.

* View your personal details
* Check your **budget allocation**
* Monitor **recurring expenses**

---

## 5. Check Statistics

Analyze your spending habits.

* View **pie charts** for category breakdown
* Understand where your money goes

---

## 6. Track Trends

Monitor your financial progress.

* View **line graphs**
* Track spending over time

---

##  7. Navigation & Location

Track expenses geographically.

* Use the map to view your **real-time location**
* See where expenses were made

---

## 8. Budget Allocation

Plan your finances effectively.

* Enter your **budget amount**
* Choose allocation type:

  * Daily
  * Weekly
  * Monthly

---

## 9. Add Expenses

Record your spending.

* Tap the **+ button**
* Enter:

  * Amount
  * Description
  * Location
  * Category

---

## You're Ready!

You can now:

* Track your expenses
* Visualize spending on the map
* Analyze your financial habits

---

## Notes

* Make sure **location services** are enabled for map features
* Internet connection is required for OpenStreetMap
